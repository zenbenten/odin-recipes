Mac and Cheese

Description

Warning! This recipe will ruin all other mac and cheese for you. Proceed with cheesy caution.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound your favorite pasta
  • 1 block (12 ounces) vermont sharp cheddar
  • 1/2 pound American sliced cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.

  2. Cook and drain pasta.

  3. Layer half of pasta in bottom of baking pan.

  4. Rip half of sharp cheddar into chunks and layer evenly in pan.

  5. Layer half of American cheese evenly in pan.

  6. Layer other half of pasta evenly in pan.

  7. Layer remaining sharp cheddar in chunks into pan.

  8. Layer remaining American cheese into pan.

  9. Slowly pour milk evenly over pan.

  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  11. Bake for 35 minutes or until top layer is browned.
